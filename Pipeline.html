Pipeline

Our clinical and preclinical programs, built on next-gen immunotherapy.

Prolium’s first asset is PRO-203, a CD20XCD3 bispecific antibody. Originally developed
by InnoCare and KeyMed, PRO-203 was first studied in relapsed / refractory Non-
Hodgkin lymphoma (NHL). The Phase I/II clinical trial in China to assess the safety,
tolerability, PK and the preliminary anti-tumor activity of ICP-B02 has shown promising
early results in both intravenous (IV) and subcutaneous (SC) formulations, particularly in
patients with follicular lymphoma (FL) and diffuse large B-cell lymphoma (DLBCL).
Prolium will initiate development of PRO-203 in autoimmune indications.
