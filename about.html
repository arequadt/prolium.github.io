About Prolium

Prolium is a clinical-stage biotechnology company focused on bringing breakthrough therapy to patients with autoimmune diseases. Established in 2025 and backed by RTW Investments in New York City, we are led by seasoned entrepreneurs and clinicians with deep experience in drug development.

Our ARCH Values

At Prolium, we embody our values in everything we do.

Accountability • Resourcefulness • Collaboration • Humility

A — Accountability

Own outcomes

We communicate clearly and hold ourselves to reproducible, auditable work—because patients depend on us.

R — Resourcefulness

Turn constraints into velocity

We reduce complexity, choose tractable paths, and adapt fast to move the program forward.

C — Collaboration

Decide better, together

We span biology, data, engineering, and operations with humility and respect.

H — Humility

Let data lead

We invite critique and stay curious—so our science improves and our therapies earn trust.
