About Prolium

Biology, engineered with accountability. Prolium Bioscience is a New York–based biotech building therapies with a culture that values Accountability, Resourcefulness, Collaboration, and Humility.

Headquartered In NYC Clinical focus Backed by partners

Our Mission

We advance patient-first science by combining rigorous biology with pragmatic engineering. We operate with clear ownership, respect for data, and a bias to collaborate across disciplines—so promising ideas move from bench to bedside responsibly.

ARCH Values

Accountability • Resourcefulness • Collaboration • Humility

A
Accountability

We own outcomes, communicate clearly, and hold ourselves to reproducible, auditable work—because patients depend on us.

R
Resourcefulness

We reduce complexity, choose tractable paths, and adapt fast—turning constraints into velocity for the program.

C
Collaboration

We span biology, data, engineering, and operations with humility and respect—building better decisions together.

H
Humility

We let data lead, invite critique, and stay curious—so our science improves and our therapies earn trust.
