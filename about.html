About Prolium

Prolium is a clinical-stage biotechnology company focused on bringing breakthrough therapy to patients with autoimmune diseases. Established in 2025, Prolium is backed by RTW Investments, headquartered in New York City, and is led by a team of seasoned entrepreneurs and dedicated clinicians with extensive experience in drug development.

Our ARCH Values

At Prolium, we embody our values in everything we do:

Accountability • Resourcefulness • Collaboration • Humility

A
Accountability

We own outcomes, communicate clearly, and hold ourselves to reproducible, auditable work—because patients depend on us.

R
Resourcefulness

We reduce complexity, choose tractable paths, and adapt fast—turning constraints into velocity for the program.

C
Collaboration

We span biology, data, engineering, and operations with humility and respect—building better decisions together.

H
Humility

We let data lead, invite critique, and stay curious—so our science improves and our therapies earn trust.
